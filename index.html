Whenever I'm feeling down in the dumps, anything cute always makes me happy! This website is made of pure HTML-cuteness! Looking at cute pictures is not only only a way to increase happiness, but is also a good way to relieve stress.

This is my cousin, Brooks, on Halloween. He's very cute! In recent years, the purpose of the Internet has swayed from scientific use to basically posting pictures of cute animals (literally and metaphorically). This theory, called "The Cute Cat Theory", is summarized by Ethan Zuckerman on his website.

There is a Japenese researcher named Hiroshi Nittono who conducted a study that proves looking at cute pictures and videos will not only improve your mood, but also make you more productive. This study is from 2012. Here is the Huffington Post link if you would like to read more about it.
